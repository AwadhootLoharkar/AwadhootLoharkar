01 About

I'm an M.Sc. student in Quantum Technologies and Engineering at the QuanTEEM Erasmus Mundus Joint Master programme (Université de Bourgogne & RPTU Kaiserslautern), and an Erasmus Mundus Scholarship holder. My work sits at the intersection of quantum simulation, tensor networks, and quantum chemistry — building tools that make correlated many-body systems tractable on near-term quantum hardware and HPC clusters.

I'm currently completing my master's thesis at Fraunhofer ITWM, developing a hybrid DMET framework for simulating strongly correlated protein-ligand systems, and building QuEnAIS, an open-source simulation pipeline.
